Vitamin B7:

Vitamin B7, also known as biotin, is a water-soluble B-vitamin essential for the metabolism of carbohydrates, fats, and amino acids in the body. It plays a crucial role in converting food into energy and maintaining healthy skin, hair, and nails. Biotin also supports the normal functioning of the nervous system and helps in the synthesis of fatty acids.

    • Fish
    • Liver and other organ meats (such as beef liver, chicken liver)
    • Egg Yolks
  • Dairy Products:
    • Milk
    • Cheese
    • Yogurt
  • Whole Grains:
    • Brown Rice
