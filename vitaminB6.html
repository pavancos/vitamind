Vitamin B6:

Vitamin B6, also known as pyridoxine, is a water-soluble vitamin.
It plays a key role in metabolism, aiding in the breakdown of proteins, carbohydrates, and fats.Vitamin B6 is crucial for brain development and function, as well as for maintaining a healthy immune system. It also helps in the formation of red blood cells and supports nerve health.

  • Animal Sources:
    • Meat(Chicken,Turkey,Beef,Pork)
    • Fish
    • Eggs(particularly the yolk)
  • Dairy Products:
    • Milk
    • Cheese
  • Whole Grains:
    • Brown Rice
