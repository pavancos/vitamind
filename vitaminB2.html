Vitamin B2:

Vitamin B2, also known as riboflavin. It is a water-soluble vitamin essential for various bodily functions. It plays a vital role in energy production, aiding in the metabolism of fats, carbohydrates, and proteins. Riboflavin is crucial for maintaining healthy skin, eyes, and nerve function. It also acts as an antioxidant, helping to protect cells from oxidative stress .
