Vitamin C:

Vitamin C, also known as ascorbic acid, is a water-soluble vitamin essential for various bodily functions.Vitamin C is necessary for the synthesis of collagen, a protein that is important for the skin, blood vessels,bones, and tendons.
It helps wounds heal and is essential for maintaining the integrity of gums and teeth.
