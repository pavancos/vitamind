Vitamin K:

Vitamin K is a fat-soluble vitamin. It plays a crucial role in various bodily functions, particularly in blood clotting , bone metabolism , and heart health . There are two primary forms of Vitamin K : Vitamin K1 (phylloquinone) and Vitamin K2 (menaquinone) . Vitamin K is involved in regulating calcium levels in the bones and helps with bone mineralization .
