Vitamin E:

Vitamin E is a fat-soluble antioxidant. Vitamin E acts as an antioxidant, protecting cells from damage caused by free radicals. Vitamin E supports the immune system's function, helps in maintaining healthy skin and eyes . Vitamin E may contribute to heart health and neurological health.Vitamin E plays a role in maintaining immune system through mucous membranes, which are the body's first line of defense against pathogens.
