Vitamin B3:

Vitamin B3, also known as niacin, is a water-soluble vitamin crucial for energy production and overall body function. It aids in the metabolism of fats, carbohydrates, and proteins. Niacin plays a significant role in supporting a healthy nervous system and skin. It's essential for DNA repair and plays a part in lowering cholesterol levels .
