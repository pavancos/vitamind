Vitamin D:

Vitamin D is a fat-soluble vitamin. It apperas in 2 forms Vitamin D2 (ergocalciferol) and Vitamin D3 (cholecalciferol). Vitamin D2 is synthesised by Plants whereas D3 is synthesized by human skin.Vitamin D plays essential role in Bone Health,Muscle Function,Immune System Support,Cell Growth and Regulation.
Some studies suggest Vitamin D support cardiovascular health .

Food Sources :

  • Animal Sources:
    • Fatty fish (like salmon, mackerel, and tuna)
    • Fish liver oils
    • Beef liver
    • Egg yolks
  • Fortified Food(food with additional vitamins):
    • Milk
    • Cheese
    • Yogurt
    • cereals
  • Plant Based:
    • Soy
    • Almond
    • Oat milk
    • Orange Juice
