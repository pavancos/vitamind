Vitamin B5:

Vitamin B5, also known as pantothenic acid, is one of the B-vitamins and is essential for the synthesis and metabolism of carbohydrates, proteins, and fats in the body.
It contributes to the wound healing process andis often used in skincare products for its potential benefits to the skin.
